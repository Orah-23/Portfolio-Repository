About

I'm a passionate and driven software developer from Zeerust in the North West province of South Africa, with a natural curiosity for how technology works at its core.

From a young age I was dismantling electronics and debugging systems. That hands-on instinct never left — it shaped how I approach software today: with curiosity, precision, and a drive to actually understand things, not just use them.

During my BSc in Information Technology at North-West University, I developed skills across the full stack — from low-level languages like C++ to modern web frameworks like React and Django. I graduated Cum Laude in 2025.

Among family and friends I'm the go-to person for anything tech — from removing malware to setting up networks. These real-world experiences ground my development work in genuine problem-solving.
